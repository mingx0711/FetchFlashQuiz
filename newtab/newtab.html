When you click this button, the extension will:
- Scan your vocab list for entries missing etymology, gender, or inflection.
- Fetch any missing data from Wiktionary in the background, pausing 5~300 seconds between each request to avoid overloading the server.
- Not all the vocabs in your vocab list has wiktionary entries, and some might not have gender or eytmology.
For this feature to work, your collection name must exactly match the language name with an initial capital letter (for example, 'German').
Existing definitions and quiz results will not be overridden.
